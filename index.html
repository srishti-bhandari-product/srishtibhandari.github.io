Making everyday decisions feel effortless.

Technology disappears when it works well.

The products people remember are not usually the ones with the most features. They are the ones people stop noticing because they simply work.

That is the kind of product I enjoy building.

Over the last eight years I have worked across customer experiences, AI-powered decision systems, and physical operations.

The industries changed. The work did not. I still spend my time finding unnecessary friction and making the next decision feel obvious.
